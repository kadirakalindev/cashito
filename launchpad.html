Launchpad

The latest project, Launchpad, has been launched. You can use the Launchpad Registration link to register. Launchpad, which means “launch pad”, is defined as a platform that helps Blockchain projects raise funds and make a name for themselves in the cryptocurrency ecosystem. In the official blog post of Cashito, it was stated that the platform will lead to the launch of a token every month in 2019. Projects that meet the criteria are promoted to Cashito over 10 million user base.

One detail in the article is quite remarkable: Citizens of 29 countries, including the USA, do not have the right to participate in token sales on the platform. The platform focuses on projects that serve two main purposes: to develop more and increase adoption. Cashito currently supports 21 projects in its investment unit, Cashito Labs.
